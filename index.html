ABOUT

UniversalDot.Foundation is focused on creating applications for the decentralized internet.

Our aim is to enable FREEDOM for each individual to create their own VISION of the future.

Through decentralized apps, every individual will be granted universal rights.

OUR MISSION

DECENTRALIZED APPLICATIONS

We build technologies that will enable a better future for humanity.

The aim of these technolgies are enable a post-computing civilization in which each individual is empowered with certain unalienable and universal rights.

UNIVERSAL RIGHTS

Personal Identity: the right to create ones own idenity.

Universal Income: the right to income for ones talents and skills.

Free Association: the right to organize and belong to any group or organization.

UNIVERSALDOT LAUNCHES VALIDATORS ON POLKADOT

UniversalDot/JOSEPH KNECHT
UniversaldDot Foundation is thrilled to announce that starting September 1st, 2021 it will support the Polkadot Relay Network by running its own Validator Nodes. Polkadot is a sharded heterogeneous multi-chain architecture that enables external networks as well as customized layer one “parachains” to communicate, creating an interconnected internet of blockchains.

UniversalDot/JOSEPH KNECHT
