WHO WE ARE

universaldot.foundation is focused on creating applications for the decentralized internet.Our aim is to enable freedom for each individual to create their own vision of the future.

nostra missio

OUR MISSION

We build technologies that will enable a better future for humanity. The aim of these technologies is to enable a post-computing civilization in which each individual is empowered with certain unalienable and universal rights.

values noster

OUR Values

Personal Identity: the right to create one's own identity.
Universal Income: the right to income for one's talents and skills.
Free Association: the right to organize and belong to any group or organization.
